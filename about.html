About Campus Cruiser

Your go-to solution for safe, affordable, student-powered travel.

Our Mission

Campus Cruiser was created to give college students a reliable, affordable way to travel — whether it's a weekend trip, a grocery run, or a ride home for break. We believe transportation should be easy, safe, and accessible.

Why We Exist

Many students don’t have access to a car, and rideshare apps can be expensive or unreliable. Campus Cruiser connects students to university-approved vehicles that can be rented solo or shared with friends through our group trip feature.

How It Works

  • ✔ Browse available vehicles or posted group trips
  • ✔ Create your own trip and invite friends
  • ✔ Only pay for your portion of the ride
  • ✔ Enjoy a safe, student-friendly ride
